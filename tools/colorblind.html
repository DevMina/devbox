About Color Blindness Types
Protanopia — No red cone cells (~1% males)
Deuteranopia — No green cone cells (~1% males)
Tritanopia — No blue cone cells (~0.01%)
Protanomaly — Reduced red sensitivity (~1% males)
Deuteranomaly — Reduced green sensitivity (~5% males)
Tritanomaly — Reduced blue sensitivity (~0.01%)
Achromatopsia — Complete color blindness (~0.003%)
Achromatomaly — Reduced color sensitivity
